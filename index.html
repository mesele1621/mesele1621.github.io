About

Plant virus discovery, host interactions & molecular diagnostics

Mesele is a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Animal and Plant Quarantine Agency (APQA), South Korea, following a combined M.Sc.–Ph.D. in Biotechnology at UST, completed in collaboration with KRIBB — designing viral vectors for gene and guide RNA delivery and applying high-throughput sequencing to discover and characterize novel plant viruses.

More About Me
Plant Virus–Host Interactions

Characterizing novel viruses and their interactions across crop and horticultural hosts.

Virus-Induced Gene Silencing

Engineering viral vectors for gene and guide RNA delivery into monocot and cereal crops.

HTS & Virome Bioinformatics

RNA-seq, genome assembly, BLAST, and phylogenetic analysis for virus discovery.

Tissue Culture & Diagnostics

Meristem culture, in-vitro micropropagation, and PCR/ELISA-based viral diagnostics.
