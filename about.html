Summary

Biotechnology, tissue culture & plant virology specialist

Mesele brings extensive expertise in plant virus–host interactions, virus-induced gene silencing, molecular cloning, high-containment plant infection assays, RNA sequencing, cDNA library preparation, and viral population bioinformatics. He is first author of more than ten peer-reviewed publications and recipient of the 2024 President’s Excellence Award for outstanding Ph.D. research achievement.

He has experience developing plant infectious clones, conducting next-generation sequencing analyses, and designing computational workflows to accelerate plant virology research. His interests include virus discovery, engineering viral vectors for gene and guide RNA delivery into monocot and cereal crops, and viral diagnosis of economically important crops.
